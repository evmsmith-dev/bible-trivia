Game Play

Game Basics
  • 10 questions per game — Time varies by difficulty mode.
  • Choose your difficulty mode: Easy, Medium, Hard, or Super Hard.
  • Question pool: 1000+ questions across Old Testament, New Testament, and General categories.
Mode Scoring And Timing
  • 🟢 Easy: 50 pts/question, 25 seconds (max 500 per game).
  • 🟠 Medium: 100 pts/question, 25 seconds (max 1000 per game).
  • 🔴 Hard: 150 pts/question, 21 seconds (max 1500 per game).
  • ⚫ Super Hard: 250 pts/question, 18 seconds (max 2500 per game) - Elite Challenge.
Bonus Points
  • Speed bonus stacks with streak bonus on correct clicks.
  • Streak bonus rules: Starts at 3 in a row, increases by tier, caps at streak 10, carries across Restart rounds, and breaks on incorrect answers, timeouts, or returning Home.
Daily Challenge
  • One challenge per day.
  • Daily streak increases on first completion of each day.
  • Reach 70%+ in the Daily Challenge to activate a +5% bonus on base points per correct answer for the rest of that day.
  • The daily +5% base-point bonus resets when the next day starts.

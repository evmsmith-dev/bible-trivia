Game Play

Game Basics
  • 10 questions per game — Time varies by difficulty mode.
  • Choose your difficulty mode: Easy, Medium, Hard, or Super Hard.
  • Question pool: 1000+ questions across Old Testament, New Testament, and General categories.
Mode Scoring And Timing
  • 🟢 Easy: 50 pts/question, 25 seconds (max 500 per game).
  • 🟠 Medium: 100 pts/question, 25 seconds (max 1000 per game).
  • 🔴 Hard: 150 pts/question, 21 seconds (max 1500 per game).
  • ⚫ Super Hard: 250 pts/question, 18 seconds (max 2500 per game) - Elite Challenge.
  • Change this anytime in Player Profile -> Settings.
Player Profile
  • Open Player Profile from the player card on the Welcome or Summary screens.
  • Edit your name with the pencil icon, then tap ✓ to save.
  • Choose your avatar and use Previous/Next to browse more avatar pages.
  • Use Player Stats to view Top Scores, Game History, Stats, and Daily results.
  • Use Settings to change difficulty mode and round review preference.
  • Reset Player clears this player's saved progress after confirmation.
Bonus Points
  • Speed bonus stacks with streak bonus on correct clicks.
  • Streak bonus rules: Starts at 3 in a row, increases by tier, caps at streak 10, carries across Play rounds, and breaks on incorrect answers, timeouts, or returning Home.
Daily Challenge
  • One challenge per day.
  • Reach 70%+ in the Daily Challenge to activate a +5% bonus on base points per correct answer for the rest of that day.
  • The daily +5% base-point bonus resets when the next day starts.
Offline Play
  • Android (Chrome): Open the game, tap the browser menu (three dots), then tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  • iPhone/iPad (Safari): Open the game in Safari, tap Share, then tap Add to Home Screen.
  • After installation and first load, the app can run offline using cached files. Go online occasionally to receive updates.
